Key elements




TSA-Approved Locks

Each of our suitcases features TSA-approved locks that can be opened by security during airline baggage checks without causing any damage.





Mesh Divider

Packed items are kept in perfect order during transit with the zipped mesh divider. An even more lightweight organization feature that allows you to keep all your belongings safely in place during travel.





Multiwheel® System

Pioneered by RIMOWA, this high-end system guarantees stable and effortless steering thanks to ball-bearing mounted wheels with cushioned axles.





T-Bar Telescopic Handle

Engineered to offer a three-stage adjustment for maximum comfort and smooth manoeuvrability.





Zipped Pocket

In order to offer more storage, a small zipped interior pocket can be found in the suitcase, in addition to the mesh compartments.
