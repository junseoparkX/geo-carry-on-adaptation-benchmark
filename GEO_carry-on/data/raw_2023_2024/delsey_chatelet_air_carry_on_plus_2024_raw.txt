Frequently Asked Questions
How to pick the right size luggage?

Carry-On: Ideal for 2-3 day trips and fits 3-5 outfits. Smaller carry-on fits overhead on most international and US airlines.
Carry-On Plus: Ideal for 3-5 day trips and fits 5-7 outfits. Larger carry-on fits overhead on most US airlines.
Medium Checked/Trunk: Suitable for 1-2 week trips and fits 10-12 outfits. Must be checked on all airlines
Large Checked/Trunk: Perfect for trips over two weeks and fits 15-21 outfits. Must be checked on all airlines
*Size restrictions vary by airline, contact your airline for more information.

How to unlock your suitcase for the first time?

Set the code to 0 - 0 - 0 (Default Code)
Using a pen, press the metal button
Set your new code
Push the lock to release the zipper pull
Write down your new code. Keep it in a safe place
How to clean and care for my luggage?

Hardside Luggage: Clean with warm water, soap, and a soft cloth or sponge. Rinse thoroughly and allow to dry. Remove marks with a stain remover.
Softside Luggage: Clean with gentle soap diluted in water and a soft brush. Rinse and dry with a hairdryer on low. Remove marks with a stain remover.
Leather: Treat with leather cream and buff with a soft cloth. Avoid heat exposure. Moisturizing or makeup-removing milk can also be used.
Eliminating Odors: Utilize baking soda, sun exposure, white vinegar solutions, charcoal absorption, Febreze, or dryer sheets to refresh your luggage.
