 InUSA Ally Hardside 20 Inches Carry-On Spinner Luggage
The InUSA Ally Hardside Carry-On Spinner Luggage is a sleek and durable travel companion with four 360° silent wheels, a built-in TSA-approved lock, and ample storage space. Perfect for any adventure.

FEATURES
• Lightweight PC/ABS-engineered material with a micro diamond finish. The hard-flex construction offers maximum strength, durability, and a water/scratch-resistant finish.

• Contemporary, impact-resistant, functional design.

• Push Button Locking internal retractable trolley system.

• Four spinner multi-directional silent 360° wheels for effortless mobility.

• Full 210D Polyester interior lining and tie-down straps to secure your belongings. 

• Built-in 3-digit TSA-approved combination lock.

• Four side studs allow the luggage to stand upright on its side.

• Top and side ergonomic GEL handle for comfortable and easy lifting.

• Smooth gliding self-repairable oversized zippers.

• Suitable for most major Airlines.

• Travel Sentry® Approved lock. Using this Travel Sentry® Approved lock allows your luggage to be opened, inspected, and relocked by security authorities without damage. For more information, visit: www.travelsentry.org
