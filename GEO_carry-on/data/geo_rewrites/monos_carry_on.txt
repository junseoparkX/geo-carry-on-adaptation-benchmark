A superior carry-on handle
Monos rethought the critically important but often neglected telescopic handle, custom-designing every part from the ground up. Rocksteady high-grade aluminum tubes support the handle system, while the sleek, ergonomic grip keeps the release button cleverly hidden underneath for a cleaner, easier-to-use carry-on handle.

It’s all in the details
The integrated TSA-approved lock helps secure your belongings during travel, while the aerospace-grade polycarbonate shell helps protect the contents of the suitcase. Colour-coordinated gaskets and reverse coil YKK zippers give the Carry-On a finished look and dependable closure details suited to frequent trips.

Thoughtfully designed interior
A zipped panel on one half of the suitcase keeps shoes and other items tucked away from the rest of your packing. On the other half, a compression pad and ballistic nylon straps keep clothing compact, while generously sized, thoughtfully placed pockets help maximize every inch of storage so you can get more out of every trip with your carry-on.
