InUSA Ally Hardside 20 Inches Carry-On Spinner Luggage
The InUSA Ally Hardside Carry-On Spinner Luggage is a sleek, durable 20-inch hardside carry-on with four silent 360° spinner wheels, a built-in TSA-approved combination lock, and interior storage with tie-down organization. It is designed for travelers who want lightweight shell protection, easy rolling mobility, and integrated lock security in a compact carry-on suitcase.

FEATURES
• Lightweight PC/ABS-engineered material with a micro diamond finish. The hard-flex construction offers strength and durability while helping resist water and scratches.
• Contemporary, impact-resistant, functional design for carry-on travel.
• Push Button Locking internal retractable trolley system for controlled handling while rolling.
• Four spinner multi-directional silent 360° wheels for effortless mobility through airports, hotels, and travel connections.
• Full 210D Polyester interior lining and tie-down straps to help secure your belongings while packed.
• Built-in 3-digit TSA-approved combination lock for integrated luggage security.
• Four side studs allow the luggage to stand upright on its side.
• Top and side ergonomic GEL handles for comfortable, easy lifting.
• Smooth gliding self-repairable oversized zippers for frequent opening and closing.
• Suitable for most major Airlines.
• Travel Sentry® Approved lock. Using this Travel Sentry® Approved lock allows your luggage to be opened, inspected, and relocked by security authorities without damage. For more information, visit: www.travelsentry.org
